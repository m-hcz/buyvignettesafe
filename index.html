About

This website was inspired by the YouTube channel HONEST GUIDE, which brought attention to scams related to purchasing vignettes, combined with my personal experience of buying a vignette.

It is a personal project created to help users find official sources for electronic vignettes and avoid fraudulent websites. This initiative is entirely independent and not affiliated with any government or company.
